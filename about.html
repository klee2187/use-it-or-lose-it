About Us: Making Food Storage Simple and Sustainable

Welcome to our food storage tracker! We are Jacquelyn and Kristen, the creators behind this tool, and we're passionate about helping you prepare for the future while reducing food waste today.

Our Story & Mission

As students at Brigham Young University-Idaho and members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, we grew up learning the vital importance of self-reliance and having a well-stocked food storage. However, we realized that building and maintaining an effective food supply can often feel overwhelming, confusing, and can even lead to more waste if not tracked properly.

That's why we created this tracker—to make preparing for the future easier, more organized, and accessible for everyone.

What We Offer

Our mission is to help you take the guesswork out of food storage management. Our comprehensive tracker provides:

  • Detailed Inventory Management: Keep a precise record of all your food storage items.
  • Expiration Alerts: Receive timely notifications before items spoil, helping you use them in time.
  • Budget Planning Tools: Seamlessly incorporate food storage purchases into your regular grocery budget.
  • Recipe Recommendations: Get suggestions for recipes that use items about to expire, integrating your storage into your daily menu.
  • Tips & Tricks: Learn practical advice for successfully building your supply and reducing overall food waste.

We believe that preparation shouldn't be stressful, and that reducing waste is an essential part of responsible food management. Join us in building a more prepared and sustainable home!
